    Mathematical Statistics

     

    Distributions, transformations, order-statistics, exponential families, sufficiency, delta-method, Edgeworth expansions, uniformly minimum variance unbiased estimators, Rao-Blackwell theorem, Cramer-Rao lower bound, information inequality, equivariance.

    Nonlinear Finite

    Nonlinear statics, solving nonlinear algebraic systems, finite deformation, finite deformation elastodynamics, mixed finite element method, nonlinear coupled-field problems.

    Element Method

    Weak formulation of boundary value problems, h, p, hp convergence, partition of unity method, approximation theory, multi-scale simulations, meshfree approximations, application to 3D fracture & microstructures.
